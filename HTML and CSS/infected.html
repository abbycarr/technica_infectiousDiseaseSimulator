Infectious diseases are spread from person to person. In small outbreaks it can be managed in a way that minimizes loss of human life. However, if we've learned anything from the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020, it's that in a worldwide outbreak without proper preparation, economies will collapse, lives will be disrupted, and the death toll will be astronomical.
